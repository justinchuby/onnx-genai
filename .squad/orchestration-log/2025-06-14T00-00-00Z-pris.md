# Pris — QMoE decode profiling

- Characterized `qmoe_linear_f32` as instruction/dequant-ALU bound: ~73% SM throughput, ~2.8% peak DRAM, 3.2 waves/SM.
- Ruled out HBM bandwidth and grid starvation as primary bottlenecks.
- Recommended vectorized int4 unpack plus compile-time quant-layout specialization; projected 1.5–2.2 ms/token saving.
- Outcome: recommendation was implemented and merged as `53f9df6`.
